Weekly Journal

It wasn't a perfect experience, yet it was remarkable. I learned that in the real world, teaching is stressful and filled with unexpected circumstances. However, it was still enjoyable because the students at Manila Science High School are very sweet and nice. I am thankful to all my co-PTs, CTs under the MAPEH Department, especially to Sir Alfie Abella, and the students for this milestone I've achieved. If there's one thing I can take from this experience, it's to be more passionate about teaching because the students are passionate about learning.

VR Training & PATNURO 2024

January 18 & 25

We were trained to explore the beneficial use of Virtual Reality in Teaching. As we continue to advance technologically, teachers must also be technologically literate by maximizing the use of current technologies that can enhance learning and teaching experiences. During the training, ample time was given to us to explore VR, and afterwards, we were asked about our insights. It's important to remember that while advanced technologies are good to use, we should also be aware of their limitations

On January 25-26, PATNURO 2024 was held for Practice Teachers who are ready to be deployed. Here, we were provided with the rules we should abide by, as well as the requirements we need to meet after deployment. Additionally, tips were given to us to be more effective as pre-service teachers to our students.

About the School

The school was established on October 1, 1963 as the Manila Science High School (MSHS). It is the first science high school in the Philippines. The organization and curriculum of the school puts more emphasis on science & mathematics.

Mission Statement

To provide relevant and quality education through its Special Science Curriculum, its empowered school manager and staff, its committed, dedicated and well-trained learning facilitators and support personnel and its linkages with various publics for the development of active, productive, morally upright and development-oriented Filipinos.

Vision Statement

A nerve center of excellence that enables healthy life-long learners with strong moral, social, cultural and technological foundation to actively participate in the development of a strong republic.
